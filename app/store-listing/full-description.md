Privacy and safety

Your data lives on EU servers (Scaleway, France) — GDPR-compliant. No tracking, no ads, no third-party analytics. Camera frames and voice are processed in real time and never stored. Delete your account from inside the app — everything wiped.

LiveStylist's Feed surfaces session summaries from friends you've followed. Report any feed item or follow request from inside the app (tap the ⋯ menu or long-press). Every report goes to a human reviewer with a 24-hour SLA. Block any user to remove them from your feed.
