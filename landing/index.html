FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is LiveStylist?

LiveStylist is an AI personal stylist app that gives you real-time beauty and fashion advice. Using your phone's camera and voice conversation, it analyzes your hair, makeup, and outfit, then provides personalized style tips — like having a knowledgeable friend who always knows what looks good.

How does the AI vision work?

LiveStylist uses advanced AI computer vision to analyze your look in real-time through your selfie camera. It detects your hair style, makeup, clothing, and accessories, then provides instant, context-aware fashion analysis and outfit recommendations.

Is LiveStylist free to use?

Yes! LiveStylist offers a free Starter plan with 1 session per day (5 minutes each), including AI vision analysis and voice conversation. For more sessions and premium features like personal style memory and session history, the Premium plan is $9.99/month.

What can LiveStylist help me with?

LiveStylist can help with outfit recommendations, makeup tips, hair styling advice, color coordination, occasion-specific dressing (date night, office, casual), and building a cohesive personal style. It remembers your preferences and evolves with you.

Is my camera data private and secure?

Your privacy is our priority. Camera frames are processed in real-time for AI analysis and are not permanently stored. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What devices is LiveStylist available on?

LiveStylist is coming soon to both iPhone (iOS) and Android devices. It will be available for download on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.
