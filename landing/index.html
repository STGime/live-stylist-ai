Questions?

The short answers. Privacy Policy & Terms have the full ones.

What is LiveStylist?

LiveStylist is your AI style bestie. Using your camera and voice, it analyses your look in real time and gives personalised tips — like having a knowledgeable friend who always knows what looks good.

How does the AI vision work?

The selfie camera streams frames to our backend, where multiple vision agents look at your eyes, mouth and outfit. The results are sent to a voice agent that talks you through advice in real time. Frames are processed and discarded — nothing is stored permanently.

Is LiveStylist free?

You get one free 5-minute session at signup, no card required. After that, Premium is €17.99/month for up to 30 sessions, style memory and session history.

What can it help me with?

Outfit picks, makeup tips, hair ideas, colour coordination, occasion dressing (date, office, casual, weekend), and building a personal style that gets sharper the more you use it.

Is my camera data private?

Yes. Camera frames and voice audio are processed in real time and not permanently stored. Everything in transit uses TLS. Generated preview images auto-delete after 24 hours. See our Privacy Policy for the full story.

What devices is it available on?

iPhone and Android — coming soon to the App Store and Google Play.
