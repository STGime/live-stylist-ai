About LiveStylist

Indie app · made in the UK · backend in the EU

LiveStylist is an AI personal stylist that lives on your phone. You open the app, pick the occasion — date night, work, casual, weekend — point the selfie camera at yourself, and a five-minute voice conversation begins. The AI sees what you're wearing, how your hair is sitting, what makeup you've got on, and offers personalised tips in real time. Ask follow-ups, get alternatives, see preview images of suggested looks. Then it ends. Five minutes, real advice, no doomscroll.

What we're building

Most "AI fashion" apps today are static: upload a photo, get a generated outfit board, share it. We wanted something closer to the experience of having a knowledgeable friend in the room while you're getting dressed — live, conversational, occasion-aware, and short enough to fit into the actual moment you're trying to decide what to wear.

The result is a focused, time-boxed live session. Multi-agent vision (eye / mouth / outfit) keeps the analysis accurate; a voice agent handles the conversation; preview images render alongside so you can see suggestions, not just hear them.

Who's behind it

LiveStylist is built by an indie team led by Stefan Gimeson, an independent developer who also builds Posta (social content scheduling), Eurobase (EU-sovereign Backend-as-a-Service), and Stupid Correlations (viral-content generator). Same focus across all four: small, fast, privacy-respecting tools that do one thing well.

Where the backend lives

LiveStylist runs on Eurobase — an EU-sovereign Backend-as-a-Service hosted in France (Scaleway). All profile and session data is stored on EU infrastructure, with zero exposure to the US CLOUD Act. The AI vision and voice layers (Google Gemini Live) are stateless: camera frames and audio are processed in real time and discarded, never persisted. Style preview images render via Fal.ai and auto-delete after 24 hours.

Our stance on data

We collect the minimum possible. There's no email, no password, no real-name requirement — your account is an anonymous device ID. You can delete everything with one tap in the app. The full story is in our Privacy Policy; the practical version is: we built LiveStylist to give style advice, not to harvest identity.

Try it

LiveStylist is launching mid-2026 on iOS and Android. The free trial gives you one full 5-minute session with no card required. Curious about specific occasions? See how it handles different occasions, or go straight to the download page.
